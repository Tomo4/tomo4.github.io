Mii Maker Mirror

A mirror of Nintendo's official online Mii Maker. It doesn't require a Nintendo Account to use. You cannot save created Miis to your account with it, however. To save an image of your Mii, right click them and select Copy Image. You should be able to figure out what to do from there. And just for fun, I changed the GUI to mostly use a dull purplish color instead of red.
